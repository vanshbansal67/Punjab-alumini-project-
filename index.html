About Punjab Alumni Network

The Punjab Alumni Network is an initiative by the Government of Punjab to create a robust ecosystem connecting alumni from various educational institutions across the state.

Our mission is to foster lifelong connections between alumni, support current students, and contribute to the development of Punjab through knowledge sharing, mentorship, and collaborative initiatives.

This platform serves as a central hub for alumni to reconnect, network, and give back to their alma mater and community.
